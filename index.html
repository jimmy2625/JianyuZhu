Versatile craftsman with 15 years of experience

Efficient and flexible handyman service in Greater Copenhagen and the surrounding area

I am a craftsman with more than 15 years of experience. I take on both large and small tasks. I always come up with good advice and suggestions based on your budget. I always ensure that the task is completed with cleaning, tidying up and removal of all rubbish.

PAINTER - All painting tasks are solved both inside and outside. Painting, plastering, wallpapering, setting up felt, etc.

CARPENTER - Lighter carpentry and joinery tasks such as e.g. new skirting boards, replacing doors, window panels, moldings, etc.

ASSEMBLY - Setting up shelves, lamps, cupboards etc., Assembly of Ikea Furniture.

HANDYMAN - Drain cleaning, Change of fixtures, Grouting, Insulation

MASONRY - Repair and plastering of plinth, facade, basement and brickwork.
